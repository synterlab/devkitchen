HOW TO PLAY

Four steps to serve the perfect dish

No coding experience needed — just logic, order, and a pinch of creativity.

1👆

Tap to add

Tap any block on the left to add it to your cooking sequence.

2↕️

Drag to reorder

Drag blocks up or down to change their position. Drag left to remove.

3✅

Use all blocks

Every block in the level must be used in the correct order!

4▶️

Run & watch

Hit Run and watch your 3D chef execute your code in real time.

ABOUT

Why DevKitchen Square?

We believe the best way to understand programming is through something everyone already knows — cooking.

🧩

Logic-first learning

Each level teaches real programming concepts: sequencing, conditionals, and loops — disguised as cooking steps.

⚡

Instant feedback

Wrong order? You'll see a real-looking error message — just like a real debugger. Learn by fixing.

🌍

Works everywhere

Runs in any browser on mobile or desktop. No install, no setup — just open and play.

🎮

Game feel

3D chef, kitchen animations, confetti on success — it feels like a real game, not a tutorial.
