HOW TO PLAY

Four steps to serve the perfect dish

No prior coding experience needed. Just logic, order, and a bit of culinary creativity.

1 👆

Tap to add

Tap any block on the left panel to instantly add it to your recipe sequence.

2 ↕️

Drag to reorder

Drag blocks up or down in the sequence to change their order. Drag back left to remove.

3 ✅

Use all blocks

Every block in the level must be used — no shortcuts. Think carefully about order!

4 ▶️

Run & watch

Hit Run and watch your 3D chef execute your code in real time. Fix errors and retry.

devkitchen.js — example sequence
1chop(onion)
2heat(pan)
3add(oil)
4add(onion)
5if(spicy)
6repeat(stir×3)
ABOUT

Why DevKitchen?

We believe the best way to learn programming fundamentals is through something everyone understands — food.

🧩

Logic-first learning

Each level teaches real programming concepts: sequencing, conditionals (if/else), and loops — disguised as cooking steps.

🎮

Immediate feedback

Every mistake shows a real-looking error message with a hint. You learn by debugging, just like real developers.

🌍

Works everywhere

Runs in any browser on desktop or mobile. No install, no account required to play the free levels.

📚

Built by developers

Created by engineers who learned programming the hard way and wanted something lighter, more visual, and more fun.
