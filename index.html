How to Play

Four Steps to
Serve the Perfect Dish

No coding experience needed — just logic, order, and a pinch of creativity.

1

Tap to Add

Tap any block on the left to add it to your cooking sequence.

2

Drag to Reorder

Drag blocks up or down to change their position. Drag left to remove.

3

Use All Blocks

Every block in the level must be used in the correct order!

4

Run & Watch

Hit Run and watch your 3D chef execute your code in real time.

About

Why DevKitchen
